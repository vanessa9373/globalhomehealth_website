Our Story

Global Home Health and Durable Services was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to bring quality healthcare directly to the homes of those who need it most. We recognized that many patients prefer the comfort and familiarity of their own homes while receiving medical care, and we made it our mission to make that possible.

What started as a small team of dedicated healthcare professionals has grown into a comprehensive home health services provider, serving our community with compassion, expertise, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Today, we proudly serve patients across our region, providing skilled nursing, therapy services, personal care, and durable medical equipment‚Äîall delivered with the same personal touch and professional excellence that has defined us from the beginning.
