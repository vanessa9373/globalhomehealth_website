Our Story

Who We Are

Global Home Health and Durable Services was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to bring quality healthcare directly to the homes of those who need it most. We recognized that many patients prefer the comfort and familiarity of their own homes while receiving medical care.

What started as a small team of dedicated healthcare professionals has grown into a comprehensive home health services provider, serving our community with compassion, expertise, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Why Choose Us

What Makes Us Different

Personalized Care Plans

Every patient receives a customized care plan developed specifically for their unique needs, health conditions, and personal goals. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all healthcare.

Highly Trained Professionals

Our team consists of licensed nurses, certified therapists, and experienced caregivers who receive ongoing training in the latest healthcare practices and technologies.

24/7 Availability

Healthcare needs don't follow a 9-to-5 schedule. That's why we provide round-the-clock support and emergency response for our patients and their families.

Family Partnership

We work closely with families, keeping them informed and involved in care decisions. Your loved one's health is our shared priority.

Advanced Technology

We utilize cutting-edge healthcare technology for remote monitoring, electronic health records, and seamless communication with physicians and specialists.

HIPAA Compliant

Your privacy and security are paramount. We maintain strict HIPAA compliance and use encrypted systems to protect all patient information.
