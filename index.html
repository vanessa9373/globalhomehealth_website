FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and how we can help you.

What services do you provide? +

We provide both Licensed Home Health Services (LHHS) including skilled nursing, physical therapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy, as well as Personal Assistance Services (PAS) including personal care, companionship, and Alzheimer's/dementia care.

Are you licensed and insured? +

Yes, we are fully licensed by Texas Health and Human Services Commission (HHSC) for both LHHS and PAS services. We are also fully insured and HIPAA compliant.

What areas do you serve? +

We serve Arlington, Texas and surrounding communities including Grand Prairie, Mansfield, Fort Worth, Euless, Bedford, Hurst, and other Tarrant County areas.

How do I get started with your services? +

Simply call us at (682) 323-1138 or fill out our contact form. We'll schedule a free consultation to assess your needs and create a personalized care plan.

Do you accept insurance? +

Yes, we accept Medicare, Medicaid, and most major private insurance plans. We also offer flexible private pay options. Contact us to verify your specific coverage.
