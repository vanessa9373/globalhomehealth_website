Our Services

Professional Licensed Home Health Services (LHHS) and Personal Assistance Services (PAS) delivering exceptional care in Arlington, Texas and surrounding communities.

LICENSED HOME HEALTH SERVICES (LHHS)

Medical Care by Licensed Professionals

Comprehensive skilled nursing and therapy services prescribed by your physician and delivered in the comfort of your home.

Skilled Nursing Care

Registered Nurses (RN) and Licensed Vocational Nurses (LVN) provide professional medical care services.

Clinical Services:

  • Wound care and dressing changes
  • IV therapy and medication administration
  • Catheter care and management
  • Vital signs monitoring
  • Blood glucose monitoring

Disease Management:

  • Chronic disease monitoring (diabetes, heart failure, COPD)
  • Post-surgical care and recovery
  • Pain management
  • Patient and family education

Physical Therapy

Licensed physical therapists provide rehabilitation services to restore movement, reduce pain, and improve quality of life.

Rehabilitation Services:

  • Post-surgical rehabilitation
  • Stroke recovery and neurological rehab
  • Balance and gait training
  • Strength and endurance building
  • Mobility and transfer training

Specialized Treatment:

  • Pain management techniques
  • Fall prevention strategies
  • Home safety assessments
  • Assistive device training

Occupational Therapy

Licensed occupational therapists help restore independence in daily living activities.

Activities of Daily Living (ADL) Training:

  • Bathing and grooming skills
  • Dressing techniques and adaptive equipment
  • Eating and feeding strategies
  • Personal hygiene maintenance

Specialized Services:

  • Fine motor skills rehabilitation
  • Cognitive retraining
  • Home modification recommendations
  • Energy conservation techniques

Speech-Language Therapy

Licensed speech-language pathologists provide specialized treatment for communication and swallowing disorders.

Speech & Language Services:

  • Articulation and pronunciation therapy
  • Aphasia treatment (post-stroke language)
  • Voice therapy and rehabilitation
  • Language comprehension improvement

Swallowing & Safety:

  • Dysphagia assessment and treatment
  • Safe swallowing strategies
  • Diet modification recommendations
  • Aspiration risk management

Medical Social Services

Licensed medical social workers provide psychosocial support and resource coordination.

Counseling & Support:

  • Emotional support and counseling
  • Coping strategies for illness/disability
  • Grief and loss counseling
  • Mental health referrals

Resource Coordination:

  • Community resource connection
  • Financial assistance programs
  • Long-term care planning
  • Caregiver support and education

Home Health Aides (Supervised)

Trained home health aides provide personal care under the supervision of a registered nurse.

Personal Care Services:

  • Bathing and personal hygiene
  • Dressing and grooming assistance
  • Toileting and continence care
  • Mobility and transfer assistance

Health Monitoring:

  • Vital signs measurement
  • Skin condition observation
  • Reporting changes to nurse
  • Exercise program assistance
PERSONAL ASSISTANCE SERVICES (PAS)

Non-Medical Home Care & Support

Compassionate personal care assistance helping individuals maintain independence and quality of life at home.

Personal Care Assistance

Respectful, dignified assistance with activities of daily living.

Hygiene & Grooming:

  • Bathing, showering, and bed baths
  • Hair washing and styling
  • Oral care and denture care
  • Nail care and skin care

Dressing & Mobility:

  • Clothing selection and dressing
  • Transfer assistance (bed, chair, toilet)
  • Walking support and fall prevention
  • Use of mobility aids

Companionship & Emotional Support

Meaningful engagement and social interaction to enhance wellbeing.

Social Engagement:

  • Friendly conversation and active listening
  • Playing games, cards, and puzzles
  • Reading aloud and audiobooks
  • Arts and crafts activities

Community Connection:

  • Accompaniment to social events
  • Church or worship service attendance
  • Video calls with family/friends
  • Maintaining social relationships

Alzheimer's & Dementia Care

Specialized, patient-centered care for individuals with memory challenges.

Specialized Approach:

  • Dementia-specific training
  • Person-centered care philosophy
  • Routine and structure maintenance
  • Redirection strategies

Safety & Supervision:

  • Continuous monitoring
  • Wandering prevention
  • Fall risk reduction
  • Emergency preparedness

Meal Preparation & Nutrition

Nutritious meal planning and preparation supporting health and wellbeing.

Meal Services:

  • Custom meal planning
  • Grocery shopping assistance
  • Fresh meal preparation
  • Special diet accommodation
  • Kitchen cleanup

Nutritional Support:

  • Diabetic-friendly meals
  • Low-sodium options
  • Heart-healthy cooking
  • Hydration monitoring

Homemaking & Household Support

Light housekeeping and home management maintaining a safe, comfortable environment.

Housekeeping Services:

  • Light cleaning and tidying
  • Vacuuming and floor care
  • Laundry washing, drying, folding
  • Bed making and linen changes
  • Dish washing and kitchen maintenance

Organization & Safety:

  • Decluttering and organizing
  • Safety hazard identification
  • Home environment monitoring

Respite Care

Temporary relief for family caregivers providing much-needed rest and support.

Flexible Options:

  • A few hours for personal time
  • Full day coverage
  • Overnight care
  • Extended weekend relief
  • Vacation coverage

Caregiver Benefits:

  • Reduced stress and burnout
  • Time for self-care
  • Personal appointments
  • Peace of mind

Medication Reminders & Transportation

Support with medication schedules and safe transportation.

Medication Support:

  • Verbal medication reminders
  • Assistance opening containers
  • Pill organizer setup
  • Adherence monitoring

Note: Personal care attendants provide reminders only. Medication administration requires licensed nursing.

Transportation Services:

  • Medical appointment transportation
  • Grocery shopping trips
  • Social and recreational outings

24/7 Live-In Care

Around-the-clock personal assistance for comprehensive care needs.

Continuous Support:

  • 24-hour caregiver presence
  • Overnight supervision
  • Emergency response readiness
  • Comprehensive personal care
  • All meals prepared

Live-In Arrangement:

  • Private sleeping quarters for caregiver
  • Regular caregiver rotation
  • Family coordination
  • 24/7 peace of mind
